Introduction

To facilitate a performance evaluation for spherical indoor images, we also build a synthetic dataset. Our dataset contains a total of 1775 rooms with 3550 images. Each room corresponds to two images: with furniture or withour furniture. This synthetic dataset contains RGB ominidirectional images, their corresponding depth, corners, plane-plane intersection lines and planes (room layout). Besides depth estimation, our dataset can also be used to empty room synthesis from a furnished room, and layout estimation.

Dataset Format

Train/test splits are provided in train.txt/test.txt. Each line corresponds to one idx.
There are a total of 5 folders, where:
  • boundary: boundary maps.
  • corner: corner maps.
  • cor_idx: corner coordinates.
  • plane: plane maps.
  • image: idx_empty->room without furniture, idx_full->room with furniture.
  • depth: idx_empty_depth->depth for room without furniture, idx_full->depth for room with furniture.
    Depth are stored as uint16 bit single channel images. During training and testing, they are divided by 325 so that they are within a realistic range.
