About Me

I am a last-year Ph.D. candidate at the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Prof. Shenghua Gao. I am interested in computer vision and deep learning, especially human behavior analysis in image and video, including personalized saliency detection, gaze prediction and saliency detection in 360 videos, pedestrian trajectory prediction, and dancing to music. Before coming to ShanghaiTech University, I received a BEng. Degree from Dalian University of Technology.
I’m a visting student from Sep, 2019 in Learning and Vision Lab at National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Jiashi FENG.
